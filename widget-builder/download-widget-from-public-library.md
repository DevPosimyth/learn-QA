# How to Download Widget From Public Library of WDesignKit?

## Overview

WDesignKit provides access to a vast collection of widgets created by other users in the public library. These ready-made widgets can be downloaded and integrated into websites with ease.

**Requirement:** The WDesignKit plugin must be installed and activated.

---

## Steps to Download Widgets

### 1. Access the Widget Page

Navigate to the **Widget** page from the WDesignKit plugin dashboard.

> **Important:** Log into your account from the WDesignKit plugin page before proceeding.

### 2. Browse Available Widgets

You'll find ready-made widgets shared publicly by the WDesignKit community.

### 3. Use Filters to Find Widgets

The **Filter** panel enables you to:
- Search widgets by name or keyword
- Filter by page builder type
- Distinguish between free and paid widgets
- Browse by category

### 4. Download Your Selection

- Locate the widget you want
- Click the **download icon**
- The widget is automatically added to your **My Widgets** page

### 5. View Downloaded Widgets

Access your downloaded widgets by navigating to **Widgets > Download** from the WDesignKit app (ensure you're logged in).
