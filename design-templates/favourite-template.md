# How to Favourite a Template or Website Kit in WDesignKit

## Overview

WDesignKit allows users to mark templates or website kits as favourites, creating a personalized collection of preferred designs for quick access.

**Requirement:** WDesignKit must be installed and activated. Log in to both the WDesignKit website and plugin page separately.

---

## How to Favourite in the Plugin

1. Navigate to **Templates > My Templates**
2. Locate the template you wish to save
3. Either:
   - Click the **heart icon** directly, or
   - Click the **three-dot menu** and choose **Add Favourite**

## How to Favourite in the App

1. Go to **Templates > Uploaded**
2. Perform the same actions as above

---

## Accessing Your Favourites

Click the **Favourites** tab to view all saved templates. Selecting other tabs displays your favourites filtered by type.

---

## Why Use This

Frequently-used designs are organized in one accessible location, eliminating the need to search through your entire library each time.
