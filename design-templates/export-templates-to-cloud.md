# How to Export Templates From Your Elementor Site to Cloud?

## Overview

If you want easy access to your Elementor templates from any location, exporting them to the cloud is a convenient solution. The WDesignKit plugin enables seamless uploading of Elementor templates to cloud servers for access across multiple sites.

## Requirements

- WDesignKit plugin installed and activated
- A WDesignKit account created on the website
- Separate login credentials for both the website and plugin

---

## Save and Export a Section

To save a specific section as a template:

1. Right-click on the **Container (Flexbox)**, **Section**, or **Inner Section** you wish to preserve
2. Select **Save in WDesignKit** from the options menu
3. In the save popup, toggle to **New Template** to create a fresh entry
4. Enter your preferred name in the **Template Name** field
5. Choose **Section** or **Page Template** from the Template Type dropdown (defaults to Section)
6. Select your desired workspace from the dropdown, or create a new one
7. Choose an appropriate category for organization
8. Optionally specify plugins used in your design
9. Click **Save Template** — it stores on the server as private
10. Close the popup to access your saved section in the selected workspace

---

## Save and Export an Entire Page

To save an entire page as a template:

1. Click the arrow beside the **Update** or **Publish** button
2. Select **Save Page in WDesignKit** from the menu
3. In the popup, click **Save Template**
4. Toggle to **New Template** for a new entry
5. The Template Type defaults to **Page Template** but remains adjustable
6. Complete remaining fields the same as section saving
7. Click **Save Template** to finalize
8. Close the popup to view your saved page in the workspace
