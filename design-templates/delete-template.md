# How to Delete a Template or Website Kit in WDesignKit

## Overview

WDesignKit allows users to delete templates that are no longer needed, keeping your workspace organized.

**Requirement:** WDesignKit must be installed and activated.

---

## Delete From the WDesignKit App

1. Log into the WDesignKit app
2. Navigate to **Templates > Uploaded**
3. Find the template you want to remove
4. Click the **three-dot menu icon**
5. Select **Delete**
6. Confirm by clicking **Yes** in the popup

---

## Delete From the Plugin

The process is identical through the **My Templates** page of the WDesignKit plugin.

---

## Important Notes

- **Downloaded templates:** To delete downloaded templates, go to **Templates > My Templates** and follow the same steps
- **Limitation:** Downloaded templates **cannot** be deleted from the WDesignKit plugin — this must be done through the app

---

## Delete a Pagekit

The same process applies to website kits — navigate to the kit, open the three-dot menu, and select **Delete**.
