# How Does the Copy Paste Feature Work in Bricks?

## Overview

The copy and paste feature in Bricks streamlines the process of transferring content between websites, eliminating the need for manual recreation.

---

## Step-By-Step Guide: Copy Content from One Domain to Another

1. **Open the source domain** and navigate to the page containing the desired content
2. **Browse available styles** by scrolling down and select the style you want to copy
3. **Click the Copy button** located on the right side of your chosen style
4. **Wait for processing** — you'll see a processing indicator while the site handles your request
5. **Confirm completion** — a "Copied" message will appear when finished
6. **Open your site** in the Bricks editor
7. **Add a new element** by clicking the **+** icon
8. **Paste the content** using `Ctrl+V` or right-click → Paste
9. **Customize as needed** to match your site's design and layout

---

## Important Note

Make sure the element style you are copying from the live site is from an element that is **already installed on your site**. For example, if copying a Marquee Logo Wall widget style, that widget must be installed on your site to display properly.
