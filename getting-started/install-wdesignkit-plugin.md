# How to Install WDesignKit WordPress Plugin?

## Install and Activate WDesignKit Plugin

1. From WordPress Dashboard, navigate to **Plugins > Add Plugin**
2. Search for **"WDesignKit"** in the search field
3. Click the **Install Now** button on the WDesignKit plugin
4. Once installed, click the **Activate** button

The WDesignKit Dashboard appears in your WordPress panel after activation.

---

## How to Check If a Widget Is Free or Pro

WDesignKit offers both free and premium widgets. You can see the full list of widgets and identify premium items by a **PRO** tag — unmarked widgets are free.

**Note:** Premium widgets require an active Pro license to function.

---

## How to Sign Up or Upgrade to a Pro Plan

**For Free Widgets:**
- Click **Sign Up** in the plugin
- Create a free account to access basic features

**For Pro Widgets:**
1. Visit the Pricing page from the plugin or website
2. Choose from three plans: Starter, Professional (Most Popular), or Studio
3. Click **Buy Now** under your preferred plan
4. Enter billing details, email, and payment information
5. Apply any promo codes if available
6. Click **Complete Purchase**
7. Download the updated plugin version
8. Copy your License Key
9. Go to **WDesignKit Plugin Panel > Settings > Manage License**
10. Paste the license key and click **Activate**

---

## How to Download a Widget by Name or Widget ID

**By Name:** Search widgets in the Browse Widgets section by their full name (e.g., Sparkle Text Widget)

**By ID:** Hover over a widget name to see its URL containing the unique ID (e.g., Sparkle Text Widget = 12059). Enter this ID in the search bar.

**To Download:** Click the Download Icon to add the widget to your library.

---

## How to Use WDesignKit Widgets in Page Builders

### Elementor
- Open the page in Elementor Editor
- Search for the widget by name or browse the WDesignKit category
- Drag and drop into your design

### Gutenberg (Nexter)
- Open the page in Block Editor
- Click **Add Block (+)**
- Drag and drop your block into the design

### Bricks Builder
- Open your page in Bricks Builder
- Click the **(+)** icon to add elements
- Search, then drag and drop to your layout

## Templates (Browse Templates & My Templates)

Templates provide complete website designs for rapid deployment.

1. Open WDesignKit Dashboard
2. Go to **Templates > Browse Templates**
3. Browse available template kits

**Filter Options:**
- AI Compatible templates
- Page Builder support (Elementor, Gutenberg, Bricks)
- Free or Pro templates
- Template Type (Page Kits, Full Pages, Sections)

Click **Import/Download** to use templates instantly.

---

## Snippets (Browse Snippets & My Snippets)

Snippets add custom functionality without additional plugins.

1. Go to **WDesignKit > Snippets > Browse Snippets**
2. Explore available snippets

**Categories:** General, Admin, Performance, Security

**Common uses:** Disable automatic updates, remove WordPress version number, enable SVG uploads, improve website security.

---

## Settings (Manage Licence & General Settings)

### Manage Licence (License Activation)

1. Go to **WDesignKit > Settings > Manage Licence**
2. Enter your License Key
3. Click **Activate**

All Pro features, templates, and widgets unlock instantly.

### General Settings (Customization)

Navigate to **WDesignKit Dashboard > Settings > General Settings**

**Available Options:**
- **Features Manager** — Enable/disable specific features
- **Design Templates** — Customize template-related settings
- **Widget Builders** — Manage builder options and compatibility
- **Remove Database** — Clean up WDesignKit data
- **Roll Back** — Revert to previous versions
- **White Label (Pro)** — Rebrand with your own name
