# How to Add Templates, Widgets, and Snippets in a Workspace

## Overview

WDesignKit allows users to organize design resources by adding templates, widgets, and snippets to custom workspaces through multiple methods.

---

## Adding Templates

### Via WDesignKit Plugin

**During creation:**
- In the save popup, select your workspace from the **Select Workspace** dropdown
- Use **+ Add New Workspace** to create a new one on the fly

**For existing templates:**
1. Navigate to **My Templates**
2. Locate the desired template
3. Click the **three-dot menu**
4. Select **Copy to Workspace**
5. Choose the target workspace and confirm

### Via WDesignKit Web App

1. Log into the WDesignKit app
2. Go to **Templates > Uploaded**
3. Find the template, open the **three-dot menu**
4. Click **Copy to Workspace**
5. Select your workspace and complete the copy

> **Tip:** Users with multiple workspaces can easily transfer templates between them.

---

## Adding Widgets

Widgets are manageable exclusively through the **WDesignKit web app** (not the plugin).

1. Navigate to **Widgets > Uploaded**
2. Locate your desired widget
3. Open the **three-dot menu**
4. Select **Copy to Workspace**
5. Choose your workspace and click **Copy In Workspace**

---

## Adding Snippets

Follow the same pattern as templates — access **Snippets > My Snippet**, use the three-dot menu, and select **Copy to Workspace**.
