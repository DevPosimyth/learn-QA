# How to Create a Pagekit From Sections and Full Pages

## Overview

The Pagekit feature enables users to combine existing sections and full-page templates created with Elementor or Gutenberg into unified kits. This helps maintain design consistency across websites.

## Prerequisites

- Active WDesignKit account
- Existing page or section templates in your **My Templates** page

---

## Steps to Create a Pagekit

### 1. Access the Creation Form

From your Dashboard, navigate to **Templates > Create New Kit** to open the Add New Kit form.

### 2. Complete Basic Information

| Field | Required |
|---|---|
| **Title** | ✅ Required |
| **Short Introduction** | Optional |
| **Description** | Optional |
| **Features** | Optional |
| **Demo URLs** | Optional (general, Elementor, Gutenberg, Figma) |

### 3. Configure Visibility

Use the **Visibility** dropdown:
- **Private** — Restricted to your workspace only *(default)*
- **Public** — Accessible to all WDesignKit users

### 4. Manage Technical Details (Manage Tab)

- **Page Builder** — Select Elementor or Gutenberg (Elementor is default)
- **Plugin List** — Choose required plugins
- **Section dropdown** — Add section templates
- **Page dropdown** — Add full-page templates

### 5. Organize and Present

- Select **categories and tags** for searchability
- Upload a **featured image** for visual representation

### 6. Publish

Click **Save**. You'll be redirected to **My Uploaded**, where your pagekit appears.

---

## Accessing Your Pagekit

Navigate to **My Templates > Kits tab** in the WDesignKit plugin to view your published pagekit.
