# How to Get License Key for WDesignKit Activation

## Overview

To utilize WDesignKit's templates, widgets, blocks, and widget builder features, account activation is required. The platform supports multiple key types.

## Available Key Types

- WDesignKit
- WDesignKit Extra AI Credits
- WDesignKit Extra Storage Credits
- The Plus Addons for Elementor – Pro
- Nexter Blocks – Pro
- UiChemy – Pro

---

## WDesignKit License Key

Visit the [Pricing Page](https://wdesignkit.com/pricing) and select a plan.

**Free Plan:**
1. Click **Start Free**
2. Log in or sign up — begin using WDesignKit immediately

**Pro Plan:**
1. Click **Buy Now** for your chosen plan
2. Complete purchase through the Posimyth Store
3. Find your key anytime in your [Posimyth Store](https://store.posimyth.com/login/) dashboard

### Activate WDesignKit Key

1. In WDesignKit, navigate to **Licence → Activate** from the sidebar
2. Paste your license key in the **Add License Key** field
3. Select **WDesignKit** from the Product Dropdown
4. Click **Activate Key**

---

## The Plus Addons for Elementor Key

1. Log into your [Posimyth Store](https://store.posimyth.com/) account
2. Find the license key under **The Plus Addons for Elementor** label in your Dashboard

### Activate

1. Go to **Licence → Activate** in WDesignKit
2. Paste your key and select **The Plus Addons for Elementor** from the dropdown
3. Click **Activate Key**

---

## Nexter Blocks Key

1. Log into your Posimyth Store account
2. Find the license key under **Nexter Blocks** in Dashboard

### Activate

1. Go to **Licence → Activate** in WDesignKit
2. Paste your key and select **Nexter Blocks** from the dropdown
3. Click **Activate Key**

> **Important:** Activating both The Plus Addons for Elementor and Nexter Blocks licenses grants access to all PRO templates of WDesignKit. Activating all three products simultaneously grants an additional 200 credits.

---

## UiChemy Key

1. Log into your Posimyth Store account
2. Find the license key under **UiChemy** in Dashboard

### Activate

1. Go to **Licence → Activate** in WDesignKit
2. Paste your key and select **UiChemy** from the dropdown
3. Click **Activate Key**

> **Note:** UiChemy Pro licenses include 100 credits and access to Pro Figma files.
