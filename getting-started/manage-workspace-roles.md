# How to Manage Workspace Roles and Permissions in WDesignKit

## Overview

WDesignKit allows workspace owners to control team access through role-based assignments. Each member receives permissions tied to their designated role.

---

## The Three Available Roles

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full control — billing, members, content, settings |
| **Editor** | Create/modify templates, widgets, snippets; no member management |
| **Subscriber** | View-only access; no editing rights |

---

## Steps to Assign or Change a Role

1. Log into your WDesignKit account
2. Navigate to **Workspace** from the left sidebar
3. Select your target workspace
4. Open the **Members Management Panel**
5. Locate the user and click their **role dropdown**
6. Select the appropriate permission level
7. Click **Save** — changes take effect immediately
