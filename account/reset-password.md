# How to Reset Password of WDesignKit Account?

## Overview

Resetting your WDesignKit account password is a straightforward and secure process. You can update your password to something stronger or change it for any reason.

**Requirement:** This feature is part of WDesignKit. Ensure it's installed and activated.

**Note:** Password reset can only be done through the WDesignKit web application.

---

## Reset Password (When Logged In)

Follow these steps to change your password:

1. Log into your WDesignKit account and navigate to **Profile** from the Dashboard
2. On the Manage Profile page, click the **Reset Password** button to open the reset form
3. Enter your new password and click **Save**

---

## Forgot Password Recovery

If you've forgotten your password, you can still reset it easily:

1. Go to the login page (from either the WDesignKit app or plugin)
2. Click the **Forgot Password** link
3. Enter your registered email address in the **Email** field
4. Click the **Reset Now** button

You'll receive a password reset link via email. Click that link to complete the password reset process.
