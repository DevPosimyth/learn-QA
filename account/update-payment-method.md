# How to Update Your Payment Method

## Overview

Maintaining an updated payment method in your POSIMYTH Store account is crucial for ensuring continuous, secure transactions. An active payment method allows products to renew properly and ensures you receive uninterrupted updates and support.

You can update your payment method if your credit card has expired or if you wish to use a different card. The process depends on which payment system you originally used: PayPal or Credit Card (Stripe).

---

## Did You Purchase Using PayPal?

If you made your initial payment through PayPal, you can only modify your payment method through the PayPal dashboard itself, not through your POSIMYTH Store account.

For assistance, refer to PayPal support documentation or contact PayPal Support directly. You can also reach out to the POSIMYTH Helpdesk for further help.

---

## Did You Purchase Using a Credit Card (Stripe)?

If you used a credit or debit card through Stripe, you can update your payment method directly in your account.

### Steps to Update Your Payment Method

1. Log in to your POSIMYTH Store account
2. From your Dashboard, navigate to **Manage Subscriptions**
3. Locate your subscription plan and click the **Update Payment Method** button
4. Select the **Add New Card** option and enter your new card details
5. Click the **Update Payment Method** button to confirm the change

---

## Need Additional Help?

If you encounter any issues updating your payment method or have other concerns, contact the support team at the POSIMYTH Helpdesk.
