# How to White Label WDesignKit with Your Brand Name

## Overview

The White Label feature allows you to customize the WDesignKit plugin with your own branding. This feature is available with both the Studio and Lifetime plans.

## Steps to White Label WDesignKit

### 1. Access White Label Settings

Navigate to **WDesignKit > Settings > General Settings** from your WordPress Dashboard.

### 2. Open Customization Panel

In the White Label section, click the **Customize** button to open the configuration popup.

### 3. Fill in Brand Information

Complete the following fields in the form:

- **Plugin Name** — Replace the default plugin name displayed in WordPress menus and plugin pages
- **Plugin Description** — Add custom description text shown on the plugin page
- **Developer/Agency** — Enter your brand name for display on the plugin page
- **Website URL** — Add your brand's website link
- **Plus Icon/Logo** — Upload an icon to appear in WordPress menus and WDesignKit pages

### 4. Configure Plugin Settings

Enable optional management features:

- **Hide all Help Links?** — Removes support links (except brand name) from the plugin page
- **Hide all Plugin Updates related News?** — Conceals future update banners and news notifications
- **Hide Licence Key Tab?** — Restricts client access to the license page
- **Hide Plugin RollBack Tab?** — Removes the Roll Back option from General Settings
- **Enable Force Disable Options** — Completely removes the White Label card to prevent client modifications

### 5. Save Changes

Click **Save Settings** to apply your customization. The page will reload to display the changes.

## Additional Actions

### Reset White Label Settings

Click the **Reset** button within the White Label popup to restore original branding.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Will I receive updates after white labeling?

Yes. Updates continue automatically without affecting your white label customization.

### How do I hide the white label option?

Enable the "Enable Force Disable Options" checkbox and save settings. To restore access, deactivate and reactivate the plugin.

### How can I revert white labeling?

Navigate to **WDesignKit > Settings > General Settings**, access the White Label section, and click **Reset** to restore default branding.
