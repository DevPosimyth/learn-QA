# How to Upgrade the Current Plan to Higher in WDesignKit?

## Overview

Upgrading your WDesignKit plan is a straightforward process. The system allows upgrades from lower to higher yearly plans, yearly to lifetime plans, or lower to higher lifetime tiers.

## Steps to Upgrade Your Plan

1. **Log in** to your Posimyth Store account
2. **Click the Upgrade License button** from your Dashboard
3. **Select your upgrade option** — Choose from available upgrade paths based on your current purchase
4. **Review the checkout page** to see the final amount you'll owe
5. **Complete payment** of the difference to finalize your upgrade

## Price Calculation for Yearly Plan Upgrades

### How Cost Works for Yearly Subscriptions

When transitioning from a lower yearly plan to a higher one, you pay the difference between the two plans, adjusted for time already used.

**Example Calculation:**

- Original plan: Starter at $39/year (purchased January 1, 2025)
- New plan: Studio at $99/year
- Upgrade date: June 1, 2025 (6 months elapsed)

The calculation breaks down as:

- Used amount deducted: $39 / 2 = $19.50
- New price difference: $99 − $19.50 = $79.50
- **Amount owed: $79.50**

Early upgrades result in lower costs since less of your original subscription has been consumed.
