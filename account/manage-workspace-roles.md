# How to Manage Workspace Roles and Permissions in WDesignKit

## Overview

WDesignKit allows workspace owners to control team access through role-based assignments. Each member receives permissions tied to their designated role.

---

## The Three Available Roles

### Owner
- Manages all workspace aspects, including billing and subscriptions
- Controls member access and role assignments
- Full authority over content publication and workspace settings

### Editor
- Can develop and modify templates, widgets, and snippets
- Restricted from member management and billing access
- Limited publishing control compared to owners

### Subscriber
- View-only access to the workspace's content
- Can stay informed through updates without editing capabilities
- Ideal for stakeholders who need visibility but not modification rights

---

## Steps to Assign or Change Roles

1. Log into your WDesignKit account
2. Navigate to Workspace settings via the left sidebar
3. Select your target workspace
4. Open the **Members Management Panel**
5. Locate the user and click their **role dropdown**
6. Select the appropriate permission level
7. Click **Save** for immediate effect

---

## Key Takeaway

The role system streamlines team collaboration by automatically assigning permissions based on each member's designated level, eliminating manual permission configuration.
