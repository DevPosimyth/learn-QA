# How to Manage Licence in WDesignKit?

## WDesignKit Credit System

WDesignKit operates using credits required to create templates, widgets, and workspaces. Access your credit information by navigating to **Settings > Manage Licence** in the WDesignKit dashboard. Beta users receive 400 credits upon signup.

### Credits Required

- **Template:** 2 credits per template
- **Widget:** 5 credits per widget
- **Workspace:** 10 credits per workspace

### Usage Limits

- **Templates:** 140 templates
- **Widgets:** 20 widgets
- **Workspaces:** 2 workspaces

When credits are depleted, purchase additional credits via the "Go Pro" button, or delete existing templates, widgets, or workspaces to free up credits.

## Manage Licence

Pro users of WDesignKit, The Plus Addons for Elementor, Nexter Blocks, or UiChemy receive bonus credits:

- **WDesignKit Pro:** 1,000 credits
- **The Plus Addons For Elementor Pro:** 100 credits
- **Nexter Blocks Pro:** 100 credits
- **UiChemy Pro:** 100 credits

### Adding a Licence Key

1. Select the product from the "Enter Licence Key" dropdown
2. Enter your license key
3. Click **Activate**

The bonus credits are added automatically, upgrading your account to PRO status and removing template, widget, and workspace limits.

### Accessing Pro Figma Templates

Activate a UiChemy Pro license key to access Figma templates. Select UiChemy from the dropdown, enter your key, choose your plan, and click **Activate**. This grants 100 credits and full template access.

### When Credits Run Out

If credits are exhausted while creating new items, existing resources deactivate based on creation date — oldest first. Only deactivated items can be deleted. Reactivate deactivated resources by purchasing additional credits or removing existing ones.
