# Difference Between WDesignKit Account and POSIMYTH Store Account

## Overview

To utilize the professional version of WDesignKit, users must establish two distinct accounts. This document clarifies the purpose and function of each.

## Account Requirements

### POSIMYTH Store Account

The first account needed is on the POSIMYTH Store website. This platform serves as the marketplace where users can purchase the WDesignKit pro license.

**Purpose:** Handles all purchasing and licensing transactions for the pro version.

### WDesignKit Account

The second required account is registered directly on the WDesignKit platform. This account allows you to access, manage, and utilize the features of WDesignKit from both your WordPress site and the WDesignKit website.

**Purpose:** Provides access to platform features and resource management across multiple locations.

## Important Note

Users accessing only the free version of WDesignKit need not create both accounts — you only need to create an account in WDesignKit.

## Additional Benefit

Existing customers with pro licenses for other POSIMYTH products can transfer value, as they may get extra credit in WDesignKit.
