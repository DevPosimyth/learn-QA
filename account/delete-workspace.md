# How to Delete a Workspace in WDesignKit

## Overview

WDesignKit allows workspace owners to permanently remove workspaces they no longer need.

---

## Deletion Steps

1. **Log in** to your WDesignKit app account
2. **Navigate** to the Workspace section
3. **Locate** the workspace you wish to delete
4. **Click** the three-dot menu icon next to it
5. **Select** the Delete option
6. **Confirm** by clicking **Yes** in the popup dialog

The same procedure applies within the WDesignKit WordPress plugin's Workspace page.

---

## Important Limitation

> **You can't delete a workspace that is shared with you.**

Only the original workspace owner can remove it. Users with shared access cannot perform deletion.
