# How to Use Credits in WDesignKit

## Overview

The credits system in WDesignKit allows users to access additional resources for storage and AI-powered features. Credits can be purchased from the pricing page and used according to individual needs.

## How to Find Credit Plans in WDesignKit

1. Go to the WDesignKit Pricing page
2. Look for the **View Credit Plans** section
3. Click **View Credit Plans** to expand available options
4. You will see two types of credits:
   - Storage Credits
   - AI Credits
5. Choose the credit type you need and select the plan that fits your requirements

---

## How to Use Storage Credits

Storage Credits are used for storing files, designs, assets, and other resources inside your WDesignKit workspace. These credits are billed yearly and increase storage capacity.

### Available Storage Credit Plans

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| 300 Storage Credits | $29 / Yearly | Light storage needs, small projects |
| 500 Storage Credits | $49 / Yearly | Regular users, multiple design assets |
| 1000 Storage Credits | $79 / Yearly | Heavy users or teams, large projects |

### When Storage Credits Are Used

Storage credits are consumed when you:

- Upload files or design assets
- Save projects in the workspace
- Store media or design resources
- Manage large collections of files

If you run out of storage credits, you can upgrade or purchase additional credits anytime.

---

## How to Use AI Credits

AI Credits power AI-based features in WDesignKit, such as generating content, design elements, or automated tasks. Unlike storage credits, AI credits are purchased as a one-time package.

### Available AI Credit Plans

| Plan | Price | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| 8,000 AI Credits | $19 (One-time) | Light AI usage, exploring AI tools |
| 22,000 AI Credits | $49 (One-time) | Frequent AI users, regular tasks |
| 50,000 AI Credits | $99 (One-time) | Power users, heavy AI workflows |

### When AI Credits Are Used

AI credits are consumed when you use:

- AI-powered design tools
- AI content or asset generation
- Automated AI-based workflows
- Smart design or editing features

The number of credits used depends on the type of AI action performed.

## Important Notes

- Credit usage depends on feature consumption
- Prices listed do not include taxes
- You can upgrade to a higher plan anytime
- All payments are 100% safe and secure
- AI credits are valid for 1 year from the date of purchase
- Unused credits will not carry forward after expiry
