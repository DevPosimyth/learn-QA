# Ready Widgets

Explore ready-made widgets built by the WDesignKit team and learn how to use them in Elementor, Gutenberg, and Bricks.

## What Are Ready Widgets?

Ready Widgets are pre-built, production-ready widgets created by the WDesignKit team. They come with advanced functionality and design built-in — just download and use them in your page builder.

## Available Builders

- **Elementor** — Full support with all widget controls
- **Gutenberg** — Block-based widgets for the WordPress editor
- **Bricks** — Native Bricks Builder compatibility

## How to Get Ready Widgets

1. Open the **WDesignKit** panel from your page builder
2. Go to the **Widgets** tab
3. Browse the widget library
4. Click **Download** on any widget you want to use
5. The widget is instantly available in your page builder
