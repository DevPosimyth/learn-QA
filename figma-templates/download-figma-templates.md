# How to Download Figma Templates

## Overview

WDesignKit provides an extensive collection of professionally crafted Figma templates designed to accelerate the design process. These templates come as complete website designs with multiple inner pages that can be customized according to specific project needs.

## Requirements

To access Figma templates, users must have an active WDesignKit account. Pro templates require either a WDesignKit Pro or UiChemy Pro license that has been activated within the platform.

---

## Download Steps

### Step 1: Navigate to Templates

Once logged into WDesignKit.com, go to the header menu and select **Templates > Figma Templates**.

### Step 2: Browse Available Templates

The template library displays both free and paid options. Click on your desired template to proceed.

- **Premium templates** require an active WDesignKit Pro or UiChemy Pro subscription
- Templates marked with the **Figma + UiChemy logos** can be converted into WordPress websites using UiChemy with minimal effort

### Step 3: Download the File

On the template details page, click the **Download** button to obtain the file in `.fig` format.

---

## Importing Into Figma

After downloading, open the Figma application and select **Import > From your computer**, then choose your downloaded `.fig` file to begin editing.
