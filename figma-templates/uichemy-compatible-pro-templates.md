# How to Get UiChemy Compatible Pro Figma Templates?

## Overview

WDesignKit offers 2000+ ready-to-use templates including Figma files with 50+ UiChemy-compatible designs. Pro Figma templates require a WDesignKit Pro or UiChemy Pro account.

## Requirements

To access UiChemy-compatible Pro Figma templates from WDesignKit:

- An active **WDesignKit Pro** or **UiChemy Pro** account
- License activated within WDesignKit (activating UiChemy Pro also grants 100 bonus credits)

---

## Steps to Download

1. Log into your WDesignKit account and navigate to **Templates > Figma Templates** in the header menu
2. Select **UiChemy** from the final dropdown menu and click search
3. Browse available UiChemy-compatible Figma templates (both free and paid options appear)
4. Click on your chosen template to view details
5. Click the **Download** button to obtain the file in `.fig` format

---

## Importing Into Figma

In the Figma application, select **Import > From your computer** and choose your downloaded `.fig` file.
