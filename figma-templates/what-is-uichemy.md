# What is UiChemy and Why Does Its Compatibility Matter?

## What is UiChemy?

UiChemy is a Figma plugin that bridges the gap between Figma and WordPress, offering users a seamless transition from static templates to dynamic websites. With its intuitive interface and powerful features, UiChemy empowers designers to effortlessly transform their Figma creations into fully editable WordPress websites.

## Why Does UiChemy Compatibility Matter?

While UiChemy makes it very easy to convert a Figma template to an editable WordPress website, the Figma template must follow certain design guidelines to make the best possible conversion.

If the Figma template is not designed following those guidelines, you might not get the desired output.

Since in WDesignKit, there are 50+ Figma templates already UiChemy compatible, you can directly convert those using UiChemy without any modifications.
